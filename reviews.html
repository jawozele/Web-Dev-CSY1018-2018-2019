freeCodeCamp

freeCodeCamp is a community that helps you learn how to code, then get experience by contributing to open source projects used by nonprofits. Generally, you learn how to code by engaging in coding challenges and building projects. With this process, participants can earn verified certifications along the way. Students are encouraged to join a study group in a city in which they live in. This will enable students to build a strong network with other people. freeCodeCamp is 100% free.

Each year, a high number of people who coin the freeCodeCamp community secure their first role as a software developer in the ever evolving techie industry. Every year, thousands of people who join the freeCodeCamp community get their first software developer job. Many high school, college, and adult ed programs incorporate freeCodeCamp into their coursework. freeCodeCamp is open source, so no license or special permission from the organisation is necessary.

udemy

Udemy is an online learning platform which is aimed at professional adults. Udemy uses content from online content creators to sell for profit. Udemy provides tools which can enable users to create a course, promote it and earn money from student tuition charges.

Currently no Udemy courses are accredited for college credit; students take courses mainly as a means of improving job-related skills. Some courses generate credit toward technical certifications. Udemy has made a special effort to attract corporate trainers seeking to create coursework for employees of their company.

As of 2018, there are more than 100,000 courses on the website.

Courses are offered across a breadth of categories, including business and entrepreneurship, academics, the arts, health and fitness, language, music, and technology.

Most classes are in practical subjects such as software or using an iPhone camera. Udemy also offers Udemy for Business, enabling businesses access to a targeted suite of over 2,000 training courses on topics from digital marketing tactics to office productivity, design, management, programming, and more. With Udemy for Business, organizations can also create custom learning portals for corporate training.

Udemy offers paid and free courses, depending on the instructor.
